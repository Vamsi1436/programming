About Our Company

Blacknight Internet Solutions Ltd is Ireland's leading web hosting company and domain registrar, providing premium web services to businesses across Ireland and Europe since 2003.

Our Story

2003 - Founded in Carlow

Blacknight was established in Carlow, Ireland by Michele Neylon and Paul Kelly with a mission to provide reliable hosting services to Irish businesses.

2007 - ICANN Accreditation

Blacknight became the first and only Irish registrar to be accredited by ICANN (Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers).

2013 - New Headquarters

Relocated to our current headquarters in Barrowside Business Park, Sleaty Road, Graiguecullen, Carlow, with expanded facilities and data centers.

2018 - Industry Recognition

Received multiple industry awards for service excellence, including "Best Hosting Provider" at the Irish Web Awards.

2023 - European Expansion

Expanded our services to multiple European countries with new data center locations and enhanced service offerings.

Our Mission

At Blacknight, we're committed to providing reliable, secure, and innovative web hosting solutions that empower businesses of all sizes to establish and grow their online presence. We believe in:

  • Reliability: Maintaining industry-leading uptime and performance standards
  • Innovation: Continuously improving our technology and service offerings
  • Customer Focus: Delivering exceptional customer support and satisfaction
  • Sustainability: Operating in an environmentally responsible manner
  • Community: Supporting the local and global tech community
